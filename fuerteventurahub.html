Hi! Welcome to Fuerteventura Filipina!
I'm Grace, a NON-EU National and along with my husband Dan, an EU National (Irish Citizen)
we have created this blog to document our journey to permanently relocating to Fuerteventura.

Our Journey So far


Buying our first
property

First on our agenda was to establish a bolthole on the island which we can use as a holiday home. We first did our research on the internet to look for properties that we can afford. Our main priority was the location. As we have already been to the south of the island in Jandia for a holiday, we wanted to check out the north of the island, Corralejo. We found quite a few estate agents whilst searching for properties but the one that really stood out to us was a company called Goldacre Estates. Luckily enough, they are based in Corralejo. Needless to say, we arranged an appointment with them to coincide with our booked trip to Corralejo. As fate would have it however, we viewed a couple of properties in Corralejo but ended up buying a 3 bed villa with a private pool and roof terrace with seaviews in Costa De Antigua!
